GPS Verification

Every report is stamped with the poster's GPS coordinates at the moment of posting. The exact place name is resolved from OpenStreetMap in real time — so you always know exactly where it's coming from.

⭐ Earn while you help

Every post, confirmation, and helpful vote earns you points. Rise through six ranks — from Local Reporter all the way to Legend of Accra.

Privacy first

Reports are location-stamped but anonymous by default. Your personal identity is never attached to a post. Only the location and time of reporting is shared.
